Welcome to TestTrack, your COVID-19 testing center finder!

Our goal:

The coronavirus has impacted millions in our world. Specifically in the US, thousands of people aren't getting the chance to be tested for COVID-19. After seeing the news and reading these headlines almost everyday, we decided to make this website in order to support these people around the nation. Welcome to TestTrack: your very own COVID-19 testing finder. With our website, you can find the nearest testing centers and observe the wait times for each one.
